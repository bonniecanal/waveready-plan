Your Continuity Plan: Where to Focus Next

Based on your scores, here are the sections of your Business Continuity Plan that need the most attention. If you do not have a plan yet, these are the sections to write first.

🔴 Training and Exercises  ·  Score: 40%  ·  Highest Priority

  • BCP Section 6 Add a training and exercise schedule. Commit to at least one tabletop per year, scheduled during a non-peak period.
  • BCP Section 6 Document your Banner outage tabletop scenario and intended participants. ERS can facilitate this with you.
  • BCP Section 2 List which staff have completed any continuity or emergency preparedness training and when.

🟡 Governance  ·  Score: 55%

  • BCP Section 1 Add a leadership sign-off page and document that continuity planning has been reviewed with the University Registrar.
  • BCP Section 7 Add a documentation accessibility statement: where the plan lives, who can access it, and how staff are notified of its location.
  • BCP Section 7 Record the annual review schedule and the name of the person responsible for keeping it current.

🟡 Plans and Procedures  ·  Score: 60%

  • BCP Section 3 Document manual workarounds for Banner and Gibson. These exist informally; write them down so any trained staff member can execute them.
  • BCP Section 3 Review the plan against Banner upgrade changes from Fall 2025. Flag any procedures that may have changed.
  • BCP Section 4 Confirm your activation threshold is written into the plan with clear language about who makes the call and how staff are notified.
